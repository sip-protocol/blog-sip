## Content Templates

### Tutorial
- Length: 1000-2000 words
- Includes: Step-by-step code, working examples
- LLMO: tldr, keyTakeaways, prerequisites, targetAudience

### Ecosystem Update
- Length: 500-1000 words
- Includes: Metrics, milestones, what's next
- LLMO: tldr, keyTakeaways

### Thought Leadership
- Length: 1000-1500 words
- Includes: Industry analysis, SIP perspective
- LLMO: tldr, keyTakeaways, targetAudience
